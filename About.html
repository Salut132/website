About Us

LIVE SIMPLE

This company slogan reflects our proud past and our desire for the future. This is the spirit that drives us in everything that we do. From design through fulfillment, we continuously strive to provide the best product at the best prices accompanied by the best service in the industry. Being the best and bringing the best to you is our relentless pursuit.

We have qualified designers in charge for developing new products, strongly following the latest furniture trend in market. In order to control our product quality, every stage of production is under the supervision of skilled professionals who strictly control the whole production procedures and the raw material selection and purchase. These constant processes allow GM to offer products with long existence.

Working with GM SC is your right choice due to our stable and good quality, competitive price, on time shipment and wonderful and skilled sales service!
